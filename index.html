Home

Welcome to Petroleum Product Management System. Here you can add, view, search Petroleum Products and their related information such as quantity, availability and more...

How to use the site ?

1. Add Products : Add new products

2. View Products : View all the listed products. You can also sort products based on their name, quantity, etc. You can do this by clicking the icon in column names of the table. First click will sort the column in ascending order and the second click will sort it in descending order. You can also view further details of the product by clicking button at the end of each row.

3. Search Products : Search for a particular product by name.
